Mood lighting to relax and enjoy

Mood lighting is lighting used to create a disired atmosphere in a room. It can be cosy, sofy light that makes you want to curl up on your sofa with a blanket and never leave. or it could be coloured lights that give your room a sense of romance or mystery. Here, you can be a bit more creative, but mood lighting is typically achieved with dimmable lamps, candle holders, and decorative lighting such as lighting chains.
